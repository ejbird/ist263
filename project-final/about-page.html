Why QuietCamps?

Founder, Erik Bird, is an avid hiker and camper. He, like many, has things to do during the week and can't recreate until the weekend.
Though we value our weekend warriors, crowding has become a major issue in many of our parks.
Even if you don't mind a crowded trail, overcrowding has massive effects on local wildlife, trail erosion, littering, and more.
The goal of QuietCamps is to highlight favorite outdoors spots and rate outdoors areas based on crowding.
With this information, those looking to recreate will be able to avoid crowded areas which will distribute park use and alleviate pressure on the surrounding natural area.
