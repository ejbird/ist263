Featured Spots

Tuckerman Ravine, Gorham, NH

The southern bowl of Mount Washington is known to be a weather anomaly.
Because of wind patterns that blow surrounding snow into the bowl, Tuckerman Ravine
receives an average of 40 to 52 feet of snow per year

Shelving Rock, Lake George, NY

Shelving Rock falls is a recreation area is located in NYS Forest service land. Thus, primitive camping
sites are free and because of its distance from the Town of Lake George,
this is a lightly trafficked area, making it a great spot to hike or camp.

More info on Shelving Rock Falls

South Meadows Campsites, Lake Placid, NY

South Meadows is a smaller camping area off of the busier Adirondack Loj road.
The quiet location and proximity to the high peaks
makes south Meadows a great spot for campers and hikers alike.
