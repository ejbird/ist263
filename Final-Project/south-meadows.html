South Meadows Camping Area

Located in the busy area known as the High Peaks Wilderness, South Meadows is a departure from the crowds. because of its location at the end of a long dirt road, South Meadows is a uniquely quiet area. Hosting beautiful primitive campsites large enough for multiple tents and a dedicated trail to link up with the High Peaks trail system.

Location and Weather Information

South Meadows campsites are located off of Adirondack Loj road. There is a small sign indicating you have made it. Campsites are located on each side of the road with about 200 feet of forest dividing each site.

Crowding:

The High Peaks Wilderness Area is one of the most highly trafficked parks in New York State. This park is vast in scale and offers great beauty making crowding inevitable. Yet, South Meadows has still flown under the radar. When visiting, oftentimes one will find that they are the only one occupying one of the many campsites. Additionally, even if sites are in use, they are spaced far enough that your neighbor cannot bother you.
Crowd Rating: 8/10

Wildlife Information

Adirondack Park is home to a host of unique species. However, what stand out most is the Pine Marten. This medium-sized member of the weasel family is native to northern Europe.
